Rules of the Game

You will answer 10 randomly chosen questions regarding music.

The game is timed, so you will have 30 seconds per question to answer it.

The game is scored as follows:

1. Every question answered correctly will give you an added score of time-remaining * 10

2. Every question answered incorrectly will deduct the same amount of points with an added penalty of 50 to discourage from just guessing.
